Who We Are

Sohaib Qamar

Investment Banker & Capital Advisor

Sohaib Qamar is an experienced Investment Banker and Capital Advisor with expertise spanning Public & Private Equity, Transaction Advisory, Capital Structuring & Fundraising, Capital Markets, and Project Finance.

He is the Founder and Lead Capital Advisor at Schawks, a boutique advisory firm helping Founders, SMEs, Corporates, Investors, and Search Funds become truly deal-ready with institutional-grade clarity, strategy, and execution.

With hands-on transactional experience in IPOs, Private Equity, Debt Syndications, M&A Advisory, Restructuring, Privatization, and Project Finance, Sohaib has advised on transactions collectively valued at PKR ____ / USD ____, supporting businesses and investors in successfully raising capital, executing acquisitions, and structuring growth-oriented deals.

Before establishing Schawks, he delivered high-impact mandates at Pak-China Investment Company Limited and the Bank of Punjab, gaining deep expertise across diverse sectors.

At Schawks, Sohaib leads a specialized corporate finance and capital advisory team delivering bespoke solutions, including investor-ready financial models, equity valuations, buy-side & sell-side M&A advisory, ETA (Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition) support, fundraising strategies, term sheet negotiations, and deal structuring.

His approach blends technical precision with strategic storytelling, ensuring that every client not only has the right numbers but also the right narrative to win investor confidence. This unique mix enables clients to raise capital faster, secure strategic investments, acquire businesses, and execute transactions successfully.

Sohaib's vision for Schawks is to build a global-standard capital advisory practice that empowers entrepreneurs, investors, and corporations to navigate complex financial decisions with confidence.
